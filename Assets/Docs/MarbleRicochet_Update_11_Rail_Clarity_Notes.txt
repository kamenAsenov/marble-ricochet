
MARBLE RICOCHET — RAIL CLARITY UPDATE 11

Tester feedback:
The game is fun, but the player did not understand that the outer walls/edges are bounce rails.

Product interpretation:
This is not just a visual issue. It is a gameplay clarity / affordance issue.
If players do not understand rails bounce the marble, they will play the game incorrectly.

What this update does:
1) Makes the table rails visually clearer.
2) Adds permanent visible bounce rail objects around the play area.
3) Adds gold rail edges.
4) Adds subtle blue rail hint strips to communicate "this is interactive".
5) Adds corner markers so the board boundary is easier to read.
6) Updates early hint text to mention rails.
7) Updates the runtime wall prefab material to be more visible.
8) Does not add new mechanics.

How to install:
1) Stop Play Mode.
2) Unzip this package.
3) Copy the Assets folder over the Unity project's Assets folder.
4) Choose merge/replace.
5) Wait for Unity to compile.
6) Run:
   Tools > Marble Ricochet > Apply Rail Clarity Update 11
7) OK.
8) Play.

What to test:
1) Are the outer rails clearly visible?
2) Does Level 1 teach that rails bounce?
3) Does Level 2 make it obvious that side rails matter?
4) Does the table now look more like a bounce puzzle table?
5) Are the rails visible on a phone screenshot?
