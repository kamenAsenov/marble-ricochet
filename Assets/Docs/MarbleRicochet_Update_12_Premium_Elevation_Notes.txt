MARBLE RICOCHET — PREMIUM ELEVATION UPDATE 12

Product goal:
Move Marble Ricochet from working prototype toward premium casual puzzle soft-launch candidate.

What still felt prototype-like:
- Materials were functional but not premium enough.
- The table needed stronger visual identity.
- Glass breaking needed stronger reward feedback.
- Shop/reward screens needed a more premium tone.

What Update 12 includes:
- Premium walnut table visual layer.
- Gold inlay lines.
- Premium rail visuals and gold rail lips.
- Improved lighting group.
- Better marble, glass, bumper and rail materials.
- Stronger glass pop particle effect.
- Win reward/title subtle pulse.
- First 5 levels hint polish.
- Shop copy polish.
- App icon placeholder PNG.
- No core gameplay changes.

What it does NOT add:
- No new currency.
- No real ads SDK.
- No multiplayer.
- No leaderboard.
- No daily challenge.
- No new complex mechanics.

How to install:
1) Stop Play Mode.
2) Unzip package.
3) Copy Assets folder over the Unity project's Assets folder.
4) Choose merge/replace.
5) Wait for Unity to compile.
6) Run:
   Tools > Marble Ricochet > Apply Premium Elevation Update 12
7) OK.
8) Play.
