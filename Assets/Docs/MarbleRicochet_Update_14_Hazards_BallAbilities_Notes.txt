
MARBLE RICOCHET — HAZARDS + BALL ABILITIES UPDATE 14

Goal:
Make gameplay deeper by adding controlled risk, failure and meaningful ball choice.

Brutal analysis:
1) Fail state risk:
Too much failure can kill casual flow.
Solution: no lives, no energy, no punishment beyond retrying the current level.

2) Ball ability risk:
If expensive balls are simply better, the game becomes pay-to-win.
Solution: each ball has trade-offs.

3) How we avoid pay-to-win:
Classic remains balanced and viable.
Gold gives coin bonus, not easier wins.
Heavy is safe but less bouncy.
Rubber is high-combo but risky.
Glass is fast but risky.

4) How hazards are introduced:
No holes in the first 10 levels.
Level 11 introduces pockets.
Level 16 introduces rotating blockers.
Levels 18-20 mix hazards carefully.

5) What we still do not add:
No real ads SDK.
No new currencies.
No lives/energy.
No multiplayer.
No leaderboard.
No daily challenge.

What Update 14 adds:
- Hole / pocket hazard.
- Fail detection.
- Fail screen.
- Retry / Hint / Shop / Menu on fail.
- Rotating obstacle hazard.
- Hazard manager.
- Ball stats manager.
- Ball abilities based on selected marble.
- Shop stats text.
- HUD ability text.
- Hazard tutorial hint.
- Hazard placement from Level 11 onward.
- Rotating blockers from Level 16 onward.
- Gold coin bonus integration with skill bonus.

Ball ability mapping:
Classic Marble:
Balanced power, bounce and control.

Gold Marble:
Balanced physics, +25% skill bonus coins.

Heavy Marble:
Slower, safer, less bounce, more control.

Rubber Marble:
Higher bounce, trick shot potential, risky near pockets.

Glass Marble:
Fast and sharp, powerful but risky.

How to install:
1) Stop Play Mode.
2) Unzip package.
3) Copy Assets folder over the Unity project's Assets folder.
4) Choose merge/replace.
5) Wait for Unity to compile.
6) Run:
   Tools > Marble Ricochet > Apply Hazards Ball Abilities Update 14
7) OK.
8) Play.
